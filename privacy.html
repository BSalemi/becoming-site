Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The short version: we collect only what we need to run the app. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. We never will.

Who we are

Foryu is an iOS app built to help you rebuild self-trust through consistent daily action. It is developed and operated as an independent app. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at foryu.support@gmail.com.

What we collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your email address, used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Mission data — the missions you create, your check-in history, streak counts, daily log notes, and completion reflections. This is the core content of the app and is stored so you can access it across sessions.
  • Usage analytics — we use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate. This includes events like check-ins, screen views, and feature interactions. This data is anonymised and used only to improve the app.
  • Crash reports — if the app crashes, we may receive a crash report to help us identify and fix bugs. These reports do not include your mission content.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name unless you provide a display name in your account settings.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, or any other device data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Foryu app and its features.
  • Authenticate you and sync your data across devices.
  • Understand how the app is used so we can improve it.
  • Respond to support requests you initiate.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), which is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Firebase applies industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

We access your data only as needed to operate the service and respond to support issues.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase — database, authentication, and analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (if you purchase a Pro subscription). RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. We do not share your personal information with these services beyond what is necessary to operate the app.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — you can see all of your mission data within the app at any time.
  • Delete your account — you can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing foryu.support@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
  • Export your data — contact us and we will provide you with a copy of your data.

Children's privacy

Foryu is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at foryu.support@gmail.com.
