What it is

Follow-through is a skill.
This is how you build it.

Most productivity apps give you more to track. Becoming gives you less. One mission. One daily check-in. A streak that means something because you earned it honestly.

When you miss days, there's no silent reset. There's a ritual — three questions that ask you to reckon with what happened and recommit to what you said you'd do.

Calm accountability. Never harsh. Never soft. Never preachy.

How it works

Simple by design.

01

Set one mission

Define what you're working toward and how much time you can commit to it daily — even on your worst day.

02

Check in every day

One tap. Optional note. Your streak grows. The work compounds. You start becoming someone who finishes things.

03

Reset when you miss

Miss three days and the mission pauses. A short ritual asks what happened and how you'll prevent it. Then you're back.

Principles

Built on a few beliefs we won't compromise on.

One mission at a time

Trying to do everything at once is how you do nothing. The constraint is the point.

No hidden resets

Missing days is acknowledged, not silently forgiven. Growth requires honesty.

The core loop is always free

You should be able to rebuild self-trust without paying for it. The basics are free, forever.

No ads. Ever.

An app about focus and integrity shouldn't be funded by interruption. It never will be.
